Introduction:

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a renowned German automaker founded in 1916. Known for its luxury, performance, and innovation, BMW produces a wide range of vehicles, from luxury cars to electric models and motorcycles. With a reputation for exceptional engineering and iconic design, BMW is a global symbol of automotive excellence.

BMW achievements

  • BMW introduced the first-ever luxury electric vehicle, the BMW i3, showcasing their commitment to sustainable mobility
  • BMW has won numerous awards for their advanced safety features, including the BMW 5 Series being named a Top Safety Pick+ by the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety
  • BMW has a rich motorsport heritage, with victories in prestigious races like the 24 Hours of Le Mans and the Formula One World Championship
